Welcome to Memory!

The goal of the game is to acquire as many pairs as possible. You can turn any two cards over. If they match, you get a point. If not, your turn ends. A keen eyesight and a good memory are crucial to winning.

Are you ready to test your memory?

You need between 2-7 players to begin the game. Click enter to submit each name.
